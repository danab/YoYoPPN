How is it judged?

There are actually five divisions

  • 1A - One Yoyo, on the string (Jensen Kimmett, Yuuki Spencer)
  • 2A - Two yoyos, looping (Shinji Saito, Shu Takada)
  • 3A - Two yoyos, string tricks (Paul Yath, Kentaro Kimura)
  • 4A - Offstring (Tsubasa Onishi, Ben Conde)
  • 5A - Counterweight/Freehand (Tyler Severance, Takeshi Matsuura)
